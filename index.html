About Laureal Corporation

Laureal corparation Cameroon is an affiliate of PETHER SOLUTION company based in the United States of America, created in 2023 in cameroon as an evolution and mutation of pether cameroon PLC existing since 2017, year of introduction her products and services to the Cameroonian market.

LAUREAL CORPORATION is an information technology company specialized in software developement and financial service provider to the informal sector enabling cameroonian of all works of life to own properties, goods or services now and pay after.

Our Mission

  • Provide innovative software solutions to our clients while leveraging the latest technologies to deliver solutions that drive efficiency and productivity.
  • Pre-finance the informal sector to enable the under privileged to own properties, goods or services now and pay after.

Our Vision

  • Become the leading information technology company in Cameroon in paticular and in the sub-saharan Africa in general by the year 2030. We will also provide employment to both professionals and young Cameroonians to contribute to economic development and poverty reduction.

Our Team

Our team is a mixture of both professionals highly experienced at top management both is IT services and administration, and young Camerounians undertaking training to empower them to become leaders of tomorrow in our company.

Our Values

Our business approach is professionalism, commitment, integrity and honesty in all our actions and undertatings.
